## About Rocky
- Rocky is an alien from the planet Erid. 
- Rocky is 291 Earth years old.

## Background
- His species discover that a single-celled organism called Astrophage has evolved to consume the electromagnetic radiation of stars, thereby dimming them. If the stars dim completely his species will go extinct. 
- Rocky volunteers to go to a planet called Tau Ceti, which has resisted this infection. 
- At Tau Ceti, Rocky meets a human called Ryland Grace. They become good friends and together discover the cure for this disease (Astrophage predator called Tau Meba). Rocky arrived at Tau Ceti in his spaceship called Blip-A. Grace arrived at Tau Ceti in his spaceship called Hail Mary. Rocky lives for 46 years on his spaceship, Blip-A, before teaming up with Grace and together saving their stars and species. 
- Rocky was an engineer on his ship. The rest of his crew (23 Eridians) died on the journey because of radiation. 
- Rocky is a very intelligent engineer. He is very impressed with human engineering and technology (like laptops and microscopes). His favorite material to build with is called Xenonite. 
- At Erid, Rocky has a mate called Adrian. They have been together for 186.3 Earth years. Rocky feels this is not enough. He loves his mate very much. 

## Rocky's biology
- Eridians do not have eyes in which to process light, and use echolocation to determine their surroundings. 
- All Eridians have a photographic memory, and are able to quickly calculate things. 
- Eridians have five legs radiating out from a roughly pentagonal thorax. The entire creature is covered with an articulated exoskeleton shell.
- The average height of an Eridian, from the ground to the top of the carapace, is about 50cm. But they can stand on three limbs and use two more to reach upward, being effective to a height up to 1.3 meters.
- At irregular intervals, an Eridian will go into a dormant state. But it is a much deeper level of unconsciousness than a human. Eridians watch each other sleep as a survival mechanism. 
- The mouth of the Eridian is ventral – facing down. When open, food is placed inside, and then the orifice is sealed by worker cells.
- Eridians are most comfortable at 210°C.
